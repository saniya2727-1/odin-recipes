Avocado Toast Recipe

Description

This is a delicious breakfast recipe

It is very a quick breakfast option for those on the go.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Avocados
  • Everything but the bagel seasoning

Steps

  1. Toast your bread to your desired crispiness
  2. Cut the avocado into halves
  3. Spread the avocado onto the toast
  4. Top with everything but the bagel seasoning and enjoy!
