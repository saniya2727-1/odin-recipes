Brownie Recipe

Description

Brownies are a delicious chocolately treat made by milk, sugar and chocolate. It is a very easy and simple recipe to follow!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • One egg

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees
  2. Add water, egg, and vegetable oil to a large bowl
  3. Mix well
  4. Add brownie mix to bowl and mix well
  5. Put mix into baking pan and put in the oven for 45 minutes
  6. Let cool before serving!
