Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a delicious recipe originated from Italy

Spaghetti, macaroni, and noodles are types of pasta.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pasta water

Steps

  1. Cook pasta of choice in large pot
  2. Melt butter in large skillet and add 1/3 cup pasta water
  3. Add cooked pasta to skillet
  4. Grate the parmesan
  5. Sprinkle the grated parmean in an even layer on top of pasta
